About Project

Architecture, Philosophy, and the Developer.

Technical Architecture

Built from first principles using a robust Three-Tier Architecture. No "magic" frameworks just clean, raw engineering.

  • Frontend Presentation HTML5, Jinja2 Templating, CSS3 (Glassmorphism) PicoCSS library, JavaScript (Chart.js for Data Viz).
  • Backend Logic Python 3, Flask (Server), Werkzeug (Security/Hashing), Session Management, Fernet for Cryptography.
  • Data Persistence SQLite (Relational DB), Raw SQL Queries (Aggregations, Joins), Schema Design.

My View of Your Data

Unlike commercial apps that sell your emotional data to advertisers, Crysis Logger is built with a Systems Engineering mindset: Total Isolation.

Q: Can you or anyone else read my private notes?

Your notes are encrypted at rest using industry-standard cryptography (Fernet/AES) before they are ever stored in our database. This means that on our hard drives, your personal thoughts look like random, scrambled characters.

While the system holds the necessary decryption keys to display your history back to you when you log in, we adhere to strict Systems Engineering privacy standards. We do not access, decrypt, or view raw user data manually. Your data is yours, and our architecture is designed to keep it that way.

We deliberately keep metadata (Emotion, Chain, Trigger) unencrypted to power the analytics engine. If these were encrypted, the system could not calculate your Weighted Chains or patterns. We believe this specific architecture strikes the perfect balance between Privacy (your secrets) and Utility (your insights).

Your data lives in a secure, local SQL database. Passwords are hashed using pbkdf2:sha256.

"The logic is open. The architecture is transparent. You own your mind, and you should own the data it produces."
